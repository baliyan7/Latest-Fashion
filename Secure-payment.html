Secure Payment Information

Your security and trust are our top priorities. Learn about our secure payment process below.

1. Payment Security

At New Age Fashion, we use the latest security technologies to protect your payment information. Our checkout process is encrypted with SSL (Secure Socket Layer), ensuring your details are transmitted safely and cannot be intercepted by third parties.

2. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express, as well as secure digital payment options such as PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Choose the method that best suits your needs.

3. Refund and Return Policy

If you are unsatisfied with your purchase, please review our Refund and Return Policy. We are committed to ensuring a positive shopping experience and offer a full refund or exchange within the eligible period.

4. Data Privacy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. We only collect necessary data to process your transaction and fulfill your order. Our Privacy Policy provides detailed information on how we handle and protect your personal information.

5. Fraud Prevention

We have strict fraud prevention measures in place to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions. Any suspicious activity is flagged for review by our security team to ensure your account’s safety.

6. 3D Secure Authentication

For added protection, we use 3D Secure authentication, an additional security layer for online credit and debit card transactions. You may be asked to verify your identity during checkout to complete your purchase.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our secure payment process, feel free to contact our customer service team at support@newagefashion.com. We're here to help you shop with confidence!
